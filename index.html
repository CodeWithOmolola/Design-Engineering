ỌRẸ MI
PROJECT 01

ỌRẸ MI

EMBRACING THE FLUIDITY OF YOU

PRODUCT DEVELOPMENT

Sticker Design & Indstndrd Marketing made by Omolola
Software: Adobe Creative Suite, Affinity Designer & Affinity Photo

This project is more than a sticker; it’s an invitation to explore identity and self-expression. Inspired by the Yoruba phrase Ọrẹ Mi—meaning “my friend”—this project reflects the bonds and shared experiences that unite us as we carve our own unique paths. In today’s fast-paced world, labels like Artist, Doctor, or Creator help define us but rarely capture our full story. The richness of each journey—the layers, struggles, growth, and transformation—is where true meaning lies. With Ọrẹ Mi, we celebrate personal identity as a dynamic statement, one that evolves with you.

Like graffiti—an art form that boldly declares, "I was here"—Ọrẹ Mi invites you to wear your label as a living testament to your unique experiences. Graffiti’s presence is both fleeting and lasting, much like the stages of our identities. Your label is not a constraint; it’s an open-ended story shaped by the spaces you’ve occupied and the marks you’ve left along the way.

So, we ask: How do you wear your label? What space will you claim today? With “my friend” as your guide, Ọrẹ Mi offers a space to explore, reshape, and redefine your path. Like the best friendships, it walks beside you—discovering, redefining, and embracing who you are. Your label isn’t just a word; it’s a powerful snapshot of where you are in your journey right now. Let’s celebrate our unique paths and the threads that connect us. Embrace the freedom to grow, to claim your space, and to tell your story—on your terms.

Allude™ is a revolutionary pocket-sized voice assistant created through a collaboration between Google and X. This innovative device combines Google's vast knowledge base with X's social intelligence to deliver a unique AI experience. Taking inspiration from the classic Magic 8 Ball, Allude responds to voice queries with a playful twist: shake it twice for a personality-driven response drawn from X's social media insights, or three times for factual information powered by Google's search technology. Designed for the digital-first generation, Allude seamlessly connects users to their social media feeds and the broader internet through natural voice interaction. The device's compact design allows it to attach easily to a keychain, making AI assistance accessible anywhere.

SoulSight Music emerges as a revolutionary audio platform, born from the strategic collaboration between IBM's technological innovation and Beats' audio engineering expertise. This sophisticated device transcends traditional music players, representing a sophisticated fusion of intelligent design and immersive sound technology.

At its core, SoulSight Music is engineered to be more than a mere audio device—it's an intelligent sound ecosystem that adapts to users' environments and personal preferences. The player leverages IBM's advanced connectivity technologies and Beats' renowned acoustic engineering to create a truly adaptive listening experience. Its sophisticated wireless and direct device connectivity features enable seamless integration across multiple platforms, from professional workspaces to personal entertainment zones.

The device's design philosophy goes beyond technical specifications, focusing on how music can transform environments and human interactions. Its intuitive interface and adaptive sound profiles make it equally at home in a collaborative office setting, a creative studio, or a personal living space. The intelligent connectivity allows users to effortlessly transition between devices, sharing and experiencing music with unprecedented ease and quality.

IBM's commitment to robust, reliable technology combines with Beats' passion for delivering emotionally resonant audio experiences. This results in a music player that is not just a tool, but a sophisticated companion for modern lifestyles. SoulSight Music understands that music is more than sound—it's a medium for connection, productivity, and personal expression.

Advanced features such as contextual audio adaptation, personalized sound profiling, and intelligent device synchronization set SoulSight Music apart from conventional music players. The device learns from user interactions, creating increasingly personalized audio experiences that align with individual preferences and environmental contexts.

By bridging the gap between technological innovation and emotional resonance, SoulSight Music represents a new paradigm in personal audio technology. It's not just a product, but a testament to how thoughtful collaboration can reimagine the way we experience and interact with music.
